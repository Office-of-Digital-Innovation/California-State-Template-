Patterns

Patterns are reusable combinations of components from our template. They help visitors achieve their goals and ensure consistency across experiences.

All of the patterns in this section are supported by written guidance and contain code snippets and examples.

All patterns

Use these patterns to present a combination of information and images.

Banner

Highlights an action and the most important information on a page.

Card

A flexible, customizable container for content.

Card grid

Displays relevant information in a grid-like formation.

Link grid

Shows a collection of links to frequently used pages.

Progress tracker

Displays the steps and progress through a journey.
